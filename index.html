THIS IS HOW WE WORK.

The Purpose.

We do not neccessarily need to know all the details of a project before we start development. However, we strongly believe in having a very clear purpose of the project from day one. We believe that this purpose should be set in stone. Thus, we spend a lot of energy on really understanding the problem at hand. This is done in close collaboration with our customers with meetings, workshops, and in depth interviews with stakeholders.

The Solution.

The next step after setting the purpose is to give our view on the situation. We usually suggest a number of solutions to the problem. This is presented along with a description of one or more ideas of minimal viable products (MVP). From this our customers decides on what route to follow. When the green light is given we start working on the delivery and presentation of the first MVP.

The Development.

The release of the first functioning version, the alpha, the MVP, whatever you want to call it, should be finished as soon as possible. We always try to preserve the momentum from the ideation phase. From there on we work with continious incremental agile development of the solution. During this phase we take in new ideas from important stakeholders and at the same time handle new theats and opportunities. The purpose stays the same.

The Invitation.

This relation starts with you. When you realize you have a challenge, you invite us in for a cup of coffee. It is as simple as that. Mail us, call us, text us!
